Fun Fact

According to a network of chicken farmers called The Happy Chicken Coop, it's all about the bloom. The bloom is the thin clear layer surrounding your farm fresh eggs, which is washed from your eggs before they are shipped to the store. If left in tact, your eggs remain protected for months from germs and bacteria that can be absorbed through the shell.

Baylor's Chick Inn leaves the bloom in tact after harvesting. Your fresh eggs have been harvested within 7 days of delivery and immediately refrigerated. We recommend you eat them within 2 weeks for the best taste, but they are able to be stored unwashed in the refrigerator for up to 6 MONTHS!
